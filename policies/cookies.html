Cookie Policy

1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember preferences, and understand how visitors interact with pages. Similar technologies (such as pixels and local storage) may be used for comparable purposes.

2. How We Use Cookies

PrivateHub uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Operate and maintain the website
  • Analyze traffic and performance
  • Conduct marketing and remarketing activities (where enabled)
  • Support referral tracking and prevent fraud

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for core site functionality and security. They are generally set in response to actions you take, such as navigating pages or managing basic preferences. Because they are essential, they may be used without consent where permitted by applicable law.

3.2 Analytics Cookies (Non-Essential)

These cookies help us understand how visitors use the website (for example, page views, navigation patterns, device/browser information). Where required by law, analytics cookies will not be activated until you provide consent.

3.3 Marketing / Remarketing Cookies (Non-Essential)

These cookies may be used to measure campaign performance and, where enabled, to deliver more relevant advertising across platforms (remarketing). Where required by law, marketing and remarketing cookies will not be activated until you provide consent.

4. Referral Tracking and Third-Party Cookies

PrivateHub operates as an access and referral platform. When you click outbound links, third-party partners (including affiliate networks, advertising partners, and service providers) may set their own cookies or identifiers on your device. These are governed by their own privacy and cookie policies.

We may also use technical identifiers in links (such as click IDs or campaign parameters) to measure traffic quality, attribute referrals, and prevent abuse. This information may be considered tracking data.

5. Consent and Cookie Controls

Where required by applicable law, we will request your consent before activating non-essential cookies (such as analytics or marketing cookies). You may withdraw or change your preferences at any time through the cookie controls (if available) and/or your browser settings.

6. Managing Cookies in Your Browser

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may impact the website’s functionality. Instructions vary by browser; refer to your browser’s help documentation for details.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect operational or legal changes. The “Effective Date” above indicates when this version takes effect.

8. Contact

For cookie or privacy-related questions, contact: privacy@privatehub.io
